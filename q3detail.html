Snake Plant

The Snake Plant, also known as Sansevieria, is a low-maintenance indoor plant. It's known for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low light conditions. The leaves are upright, sword-shaped, and variously colored. Snake plants are excellent choices for beginners.

Scientific Name: Sansevieria trifasciata
Watering: Allow the soil to dry between waterings. Water sparingly.
Light: Thrives in indirect light. Can tolerate low light conditions.
Height: 2 to 4 feet tall
Air Purification: Excellent air purifier, removing toxins.

Exotic Plant

The Exotic Plant adds a touch of tropical beauty to your space. With vibrant colors and unique foliage, it's perfect for those looking to enhance their indoor garden.

Scientific Name: Exoticus beauticus
Watering: Keep the soil consistently moist. Mist the leaves regularly.
Light: Prefers bright, indirect light. Protect from direct sunlight.
Height: 1 to 3 feet tall
Special Features: Colorful and unique foliage.
